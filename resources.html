Tools for Exploring STEM

  • Science

    1. http://winter.public.iastate.edu/chefs-initiative/ - Eminent Female Scientists

      Chats with Eminent Female Scientists.

      A series of two-minute videos relating concepts from textbook chemistry to current MIT research and applications in medicine, the environment, and energy.

      A subreddit for women who work in, about, or around science.

Job Shadowing

Job shadowing allows a student to spend a few hours or a day with a professional working in the student's career interest. The student has the opportunity to experience what the job is like and can ask questions too!

Research Job Shadowing opportunities and programs running in your area!

Be optimistic, some companies, even if they don't have an explicit program for job shadowing, may set you up with professionals in the career you are interested in learning about. A good start would be contacting a human resource representative. Most company contact information is available on their website.
